About Us...

From Humble Beginnings...

Designed in 2020 as an original e-Business for coursework two in COM425, Imperium Learning was founded by Jordan Irvine. Imperium, the Latin phrase for power or command, was the choice of naming this learning experience platform. A piece of coursework that achieved 71% by Prof. Maurice Mulvenna, it was discovered that Imperium Learning had the interest and potential to become a strong learning web application. Through SOSTAC and PEST analysis methods, Imperium Learning would stand above its competition by offering a unique learning experience for students of all ages.

...to the Moon!

Fast forward to 2022, Jordan Irvine would decide Imperium Learning would also enter final year, as he submitted a Learning Experience Platform with the same name as his COM668 Final Year Project idea. After meeting with Paddy McAllister, Jordan’s project mentor, and Peer Support Group 15, discussions were held on how to bring Imperium Learning to life. It was decided Imperium Learning would include niche technologies such as AI chatbots, a peer socialability element, and learning content accessibility features.
