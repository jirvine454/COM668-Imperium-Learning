As a certified Imperium Learning Instructor, you have admin privileges in adding a course for students to take. Add unique courses that teach different skills and new programming languages.

Update Courses

Updating Imperium learning courses involves editing existing courses so that they may be updated for the 2023 technology landscape. Update course content, images, lecturer, or difficulty level.

Deleting Imperium Learning courses permanently removes it form the Learning Experience Platform. Perhaps a course is no longer available, or had been updated to a new version of a course.
